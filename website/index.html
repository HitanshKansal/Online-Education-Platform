The future of Education here

Learning poverty affects almost 250 million children around the world. It is estimated that up to 80% of children in low-income countries remain unable to read and understand a simple story by the age of ten. Through providing teachers with access to digital professional development materials and a community, we improve the learning outcomes of the children they teach.
